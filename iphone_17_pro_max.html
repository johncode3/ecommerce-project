Frequently Asked Questions

Does it come with a charger?

The iPhone 15 Pro comes with a USB-C to USB-C charging cable. For fast charging, we recommend using a 20W or higher USB-C power adapter, sold separately.

Is it waterproof?

Yes, it has an IP68 rating, meaning it's water resistant up to 6 meters for 30 minutes. However, water damage is not covered under warranty.

Can I trade in my old iPhone?

Yes! We offer trade-in programs for eligible devices. You can get instant credit toward your new iPhone. Visit our store or check online for your device's trade-in value.

What's the warranty period?

All iPhones come with a one-year limited warranty and up to 90 days of complimentary technical support. AppleCare+ extends coverage to 2 years and includes accidental damage protection.

How long does the battery last?

The iPhone 15 Pro provides up to 29 hours of video playback. With normal use, you can expect all-day battery life. Battery performance depends on usage patterns and settings.

Is there a headphone jack?

No, the iPhone 15 Pro uses wireless audio or the USB-C port for audio. We recommend AirPods or other Bluetooth headphones for the best experience.
